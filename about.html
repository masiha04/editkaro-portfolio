Our Story

Editkaro.in started in 2022 as a small freelance editing service by two friends who loved storytelling through video. What began as editing YouTube vlogs quickly grew into a full-service agency handling reels, documentaries, brand ads, and social media campaigns for clients across India.

Today we're a team of 10+ creative professionals delivering 500+ projects for clients in fashion, gaming, sports, education, and e-commerce — generating over 50 million views combined.
